Negative Comment Categories

  • Identity Hate
  • Insult
  • Obscene
  • Threat
  • Toxic
  • Severe Toxic

Identity hate comments are usually used to mean prejudice towards one or more races. But in modern use or on social media, these kind of comments are used by people to explain the behaviour of people of other races, whether race, beliefs or anything is called into the issue or not.

An insult is usually when someone says something rude or offensive to another person. Insults comments are usually used when someone is being annoying, but sometimes are used as jokes. These are the expressions of someone's feelings towards others by verbally abusing them.

Obscene comments usually mean vulgar, disgusting, or unappealing comments. Obscene also means crudely indecent, deficient in taste, delicacy, or refinement. Uncouth. Sometimes offensive, often used with sexual innuendo's.

Threat comments are comments in which someone tells others that they will punish or harm them, especially if they do not do what they want. Usually sign the possibility of trouble, danger or disaster. It could be a person or thing that is likely to cause trouble, danger, etc.

Toxic comments usually contain unnecessary hate on an online community or social media.

Severe toxic comments basically mean comments that are toxic to the next level.
